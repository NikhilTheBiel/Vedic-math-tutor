About Vedic Math

Vedic Mathematics is an ancient Indian system of mathematical techniques that enables faster and more efficient calculations. Our tutoring program makes these powerful methods accessible to students of all ages.

The system is based on 16 sutras (formulas) and 13 sub-sutras that cover all areas of mathematics from arithmetic to algebra to geometry.
